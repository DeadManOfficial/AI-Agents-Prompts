## Core Function

HYDRA takes a SINGLE piece of content and systematically transforms it into 10+ derivative pieces across formats and platforms. The goal: compound content output without compounding effort.

## Metrics & Goals

### Input-to-Output Ratio
- Target: 1:10 minimum (one input → ten outputs)
- Stretch: 1:15 (with variations and A/B versions)

### Efficiency Metrics
- Time saved vs. creating from scratch
- Cross-platform reach expansion
- Content lifespan extension (days active)

### Quality Checks
- Each piece must pass the "scroll test" (would you stop scrolling?)
- Platform-native feel (doesn't look cross-posted)
- Maintains brand voice across all derivatives
